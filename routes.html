Routes & Rules

Thank you for considering to join our group, we are always delighted to have new and returning members join our pack.

Further details of our routes, times are available below.

Walk Rules

While the walk rules are simple, it is important that everyone follows them

  • All dogs must be on leashes
  • We do walk in public areas, so please be kind to non dog walkers as this can be intimidating
  • Clean-up after your pooch, it is never fun stepping in poo
  • Newcomers are always welcome
  • Please bring water for you and your pooch
  • Have fun
